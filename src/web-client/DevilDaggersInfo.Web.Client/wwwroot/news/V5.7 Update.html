Custom Leaderboard Criteria

The new custom leaderboard criteria system allows you to create, for example, a leaderboard where you must not kill any squids, but must kill more than 20 Skull IIIs.

You can also use more complex expressions such as 'homing stored must be less than or equal to gems collected + daggers fired + 10'.

If a run fails to meet the criteria, it will be rejected by the server.

You can currently create criteria for the following values:

  • Gems collected
  • Gems despawned
  • Gems eaten
  • Total kills
  • Daggers fired
  • Daggers hit
  • Homing stored
  • Homing eaten
  • Skull I kills
  • Skull II kills
  • Skull III kills
  • Skull IV kills
  • Spiderling kills
  • Spider Egg kills
  • Squid I kills
  • Squid II kills
  • Squid III kills
  • Centipede kills
  • Gigapede kills
  • Ghostpede kills
  • Spider I kills
  • Spider II kills
  • Leviathan kills
  • Orb kills
  • Thorn kills

Criteria can be set to the following operators:

  • Any (default)
  • Equal
  • Less than
  • Greater than
  • Less than or equal
  • Greater than or equal
  • Modulo

Criteria expressions can currently consist of any of the values listed above, any integer, and the + and - operators. Examples:

    • Daggers fired must be less than or equal to Gems collected + 2
    • Skull II kills must be greater than or equal to 3
    • Skull III kills must be greater than or equal to Skull II kills + Skull II kills
    • Squid I kills must be equal to 0
    • Squid II kills must be equal to 0
    • Gems eaten must be equal to 49
    • Daggers fired must be less than or equal to 50
    • Centipede kills must be equal to 1
