Custom Leaderboard Criteria

The new custom leaderboard criteria system allows you to create, for example, a leaderboard where you must not kill any squids, but must kill more than 20 Skull IIIs. If a run fails to meet the criteria, it will be rejected by the server.

You can currently create criteria for the following values:

  • Gems collected
  • Gems despawned
  • Gems eaten
  • Total kills
  • Daggers fired
  • Daggers hit
  • Homing stored
  • Homing eaten
  • Skull I kills
  • Skull II kills
  • Skull III kills
  • Skull IV kills
  • Spiderling kills
  • Spider Egg kills
  • Squid I kills
  • Squid II kills
  • Squid III kills
  • Centipede kills
  • Gigapede kills
  • Ghostpede kills
  • Spider I kills
  • Spider II kills
  • Leviathan kills
  • Orb kills
  • Thorn kills

Criteria can be set to the following operators:

  • Any (default)
  • Equal
  • Less than
  • Greater than
  • Less than or equal
  • Greater than or equal
  • Modulo
