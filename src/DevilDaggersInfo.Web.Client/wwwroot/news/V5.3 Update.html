New Features

  • You can now change your own settings and flag if you have an account that is linked to your Devil Daggers in-game profile.
  • HRTF, V-sync, and Invert Y have been added to the player settings.
  • The guides for how to create spawnsets and mods have been rewritten.
  • New simplified guides have been added that explain how to use spawnsets and mods.
  • You can change your name and password if you have an account.
  • The latest news is now displayed on the home page.
